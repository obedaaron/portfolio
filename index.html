FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before we work together.

What is your typical development process?

I follow a structured 4-step approach: Requirement Gathering → Prototyping & Design → Development → Deployment & Testing. Clear communication at each stage ensures zero surprises.

Do you handle both Frontend and Backend?

Yes. As a Full-Stack Developer I manage the entire product — UI (React/HTML/CSS), server logic (PHP/Python), and data layer (MySQL). This gives you a more cohesive architecture and faster delivery.

How long does it take to build a custom application?

A standard business MVP typically takes 4–6 weeks. Complex enterprise solutions with custom APIs may take 3+ months. I provide detailed timelines after the initial consultation.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Absolutely. I offer maintenance packages to ensure your application stays updated, secure, and compatible with the latest browser standards and server requirements.

What's your preferred way to communicate?

Email works best for detailed briefs. For ongoing projects I'm happy to use Slack, Discord, or any tool you prefer. I respond within 24 hours on business days.
