FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about working with me.

What is your typical development process?

I follow a structured 4-step approach: Requirement Gathering (understanding your needs), Prototyping (designing the UI/UX), Development (building the frontend and backend), and Deployment & Testing (ensuring everything runs smoothly on the server).

Do you handle both Frontend and Backend?

Yes. As a Full-Stack Developer, I manage the user interface (React/HTML/CSS), the server logic (PHP/Python), and the data management (MySQL). This ensures a more cohesive architecture and faster development cycles.

How long does it take to build a custom application?

Timelines vary depending on complexity. A standard business MVP typically takes 4–6 weeks, while more complex enterprise solutions with custom APIs and advanced logic may take 3 months or more. I provide detailed timelines after the initial consultation.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Absolutely. I offer maintenance packages to ensure your application stays updated, secure, and compatible with the latest browser standards or server requirements.
