What is your typical development process?

I follow 4 steps: Requirement Gathering → Prototyping → Development → Deployment & Testing. Clear communication at every stage means zero surprises.

Do you handle both Frontend and Backend?

Yes. As a Full-Stack Developer I own the entire product — UI (React/HTML/CSS), server logic (PHP/Python), and data layer (MySQL). One developer, cohesive architecture, faster delivery.

How long does it take to build a custom app?

A standard MVP takes 4–6 weeks. Complex enterprise solutions may take 3+ months. Detailed timelines are provided after the initial consultation.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Absolutely. Maintenance packages are available to keep your app updated, secure, and compatible with evolving browser and server standards.

How do we communicate during a project?

Email for briefs, Slack or Discord for day-to-day updates — whatever you prefer. I respond within 24 hours on business days and provide regular progress summaries.
